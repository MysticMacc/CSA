Project: Customer Shopping Analysis

BY: Krishna Mohan Singh & Harshit Singh

Bio: Unlock the hidden patterns in your sales data and transform shopping trends into actionable bussiness strategies. This customer shopping analysis project will help you maximize sales, predict customer needs, and drive growth with data-driven insights.
